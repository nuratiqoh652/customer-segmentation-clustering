# Customer Segmentation & Marketing Strategy Analysis
Customer segmentation analysis using clustering to optimize marketing strategy and improve conversion rates.

**Author:** Nur Atiqoh  

## Description
This project focuses on customer segmentation using clustering techniques to analyze customer responses to previous marketing campaigns. The goal is to identify distinct customer groups and develop targeted marketing strategies to improve conversion rates and business performance.

## Objectives
- Segment customers based on behavior and response to campaigns  
- Identify high-value and high-potential customer groups  
- Provide actionable marketing strategies for each segment  

## Methodology

### 1. Data Analysis
- Analyzing customer response to previous campaigns  
- Evaluating engagement levels across segments  

### 2. Clustering
- Customers are grouped into 3 clusters:
  - Cluster 0  
  - Cluster 1  
  - Cluster 2  

## Results

### 🔹 Cluster Insights
- **Cluster 2**  
  - Highest response rate  
  - Highly engaged customers  
  - Considered as loyal customers  

- **Cluster 1**  
  - Moderate response rate  
  - High potential for improvement  
  - Suitable for retargeting strategies  

- **Cluster 0**  
  - Very low response rate  
  - Low engagement  
  - Requires awareness-focused approach  

## Business Recommendations

- **Cluster 1**  
  → Focus on retargeting strategies such as personalized promotions, upselling, and digital marketing campaigns  

- **Cluster 2**  
  → Maintain engagement through loyalty programs and exclusive offers  

- **Cluster 0**  
  → Increase awareness through discounts, promotions, and product education  

## Expected Impact
- Improved conversion rate  
- Increased customer engagement  
- Higher business revenue through targeted marketing strategies

## Conclusion
Customer segmentation using clustering provides valuable insights for designing more effective and targeted marketing strategies.
